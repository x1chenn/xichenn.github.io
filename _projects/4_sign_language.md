**Research Intern — with Prof. Xiaofang Liu · Apr 2023 – Jun 2024**

- Developed a Transformer-based video-stream sign-language recognition algorithm modeling point similarity and gesture motion across consecutive frames.
- Integrated S2T and S2G+G2T architectures with CTC loss to model word–sentence correlations and improve real-time accuracy.
- Achieved ~**87% BLEU-1/BLEU-2**; received national recognition in the College Students Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition.
